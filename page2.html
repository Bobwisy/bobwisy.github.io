ADVISORY - WHAT WE DO

  • CORPORATE & COMMERCIAL LAW- We offer a range of Corporate and Commercial Law services. These services include but are not limited to Company Secretarial; Corporate Governance; Compliance; Corporate Structuring And Restructuring; Contracts Drafting and Reviews; Contract Auditing; Due Diligence; Mergers, Acquisitions and Disposals; Competition Law Advisory and Audit; B-BBEE Advisory; and general Business Advisory. At BLA, our client’s corporate and commercial needs are our priority and we offer these services for their business operations in South Africa or across borders.
  • CROSS-BORDER BUSINESS ADVISORY - We offer business advisory services, market research and due diligence services on cross-border transactions. Whether the client is intending to enter a new market or already existing in the market, we advise our clients on regulatory requirements relating to their core business activities as well as the business culture(s) of the particular country(ies).
  • PROPERTY LAW AND INVESTMENT - Whether it is for personal, business or investment purposes, we advise clients through the process of acquiring or disposing of properties, conducting due diligence on the properties as well as on sellers or buyers and working with clients throughout the transfer process.

  • ARBITRATION AND COMMERCIAL LITIGATION - Disputes and litigation are a reality when doing business. At BLA, we do our best to settle every dispute as quickly and efficiently as possible, saving the client precious time and money. Our client’s best interest is always at the fore of our practice, including when representing a client on an arbitration or commercial litigation matter.
  • LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT LAW - At BLA we represent employers, employees, contractors, individuals and corporates on labour relations and employment matters. Our approach is to offer advice that is practical and mitigates any current and future labour related risks. We set up labour relations structures for businesses including drafting of or advising on employment contracts, conditions of employment, labour processes and procedures, attending to disciplinary hearings, collective bargaining, terminations and wrongful dismissals, as well as representing our clients before tribunals such as the CCMA. 
  • ENERGY & RESOURCES - Our energy practice is equipped to handle matters such as preparing of industry contracts, joint venture agreements or production sharing contracts. We also act as advisors on negotiations.
  • INDIVIDUAL LEGAL ADVISORY - We offer legal advisory services to individuals in their personal capacity, whether it has to do with their personal matter or with a business transaction they are involved with in their personal capacity.
  • IN-HOUSE LEGAL TRAINING - At BLA, we offer in-house training from corporate governance to business culture. On the occasion where a business or client wants to update its employees on current regulations relating to a business area or country, our team is experienced in training and will go to the client’s location and train the staff on the regulations, practice or country requested.
