WHO WE ARE

Bassey L Attorneys Inc. (BLA) is a full-service business law firm duly registered with the law society of South Africa. As a fast-growing law firm, BLA strives to render innovative and top-notch legal advice and services to private individuals, companies, institutions and international clients. Our firm benefits from being South African and international in its management structure, identity and approach. BLA is founded on three guiding principles: Professionalism, Commitment, and Quality. Our determined commitment to client service translates into anticipating clients’ needs and offering solutions that suit the clients’ objectives. Our clients stands to benefit from a team of highly skilled and experienced advisors, having the ability to provide expert and specialized knowledge with utmost professionalism and integrity. As a competent law firm, the focal points of our activities primarily include Corporate and Commercial Law, Cross – Border Business Advisory, Property Law and Investment, Transactional Advisory and Negotiations, Mergers, Acquisitions and Disposals, Arbitration and Commercial Litigation, Labor and Employment law, Energy and Resources, Individual Legal Advisory, and In-house Legal Training. At BLA, we seek to respond to the needs of our clients through our professional involvement, a clear empathy with their approaches and the objectivity that emanates from the strict compliance with the professional code of ethics. Do not hesitate to contact us for a consultation. We will be happy to assist you in tackling your legal questions and problems.
