OUR AWESOME TEAM

The team at BLA is comprised of professionals with different expertise and experiences, spanning over several years. We always endeavour to build a relationship with our clients and represent their interests the best way possible.
At the head of the team is Lovelyn Bassey, the Director of BLA. She is a practising attorney, with admissions to the New York and the South African Bars respectively. She is also a member of the New York Bar Association and the Law Society of South Africa.
Lovelyn holds an LL.B from the University of Pretoria in South Africa, a Masters of Laws from Howard University School of Law in Washington DC. U.S.A and is working on another Masters of Laws in Oil, Gas and Energy Law.
Lovelyn is well experienced in diverse areas of practice and business, which including Corporate Law; Commercial Law; Corporate Governance; Business Advisory; Company Secretarial; Transactional Advisory and Negotiations; Mergers, Acquisitions and Disposals; Cross-Border Transactions; Labour and Employment Law; Arbitration and Litigation; Property Law; Competition Law; and Energy And Resources.
Lovelyn believes that every matter and client should be treated with the utmost respect and attended to accordingly. She also believes in providing clients with the right legal advice that meets their needs and mitigates their risks.
